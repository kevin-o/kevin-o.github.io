World Bank Data Viewer Application

The World Bank publishes lots of healthcare and population time series data, by country, by year that offer a rich dataset for determining how countries compare and how countries improve on key health and population indicators. My application provides a flexible tool for viewing geographic maps of user selectable health and economic indicators for the years 1970 - 2014.

Data Series Available

There are a total of 155 time series available for browsing through my application. Here is a small sample of the entire set available:

How to Use

On the left hand side of the application is an input panel with several selectable fields.

To get started:

  1. Select Time Series through the drop down selection list
  2. Use the slider bar to select the year You can also press the "play" button under the year slider to set the map in motion!

Just keep trying different combinations of indicators and years to see the data in the database!

How to Use (cont'd)

The left side panel also includes a histogram of the country data for the selected time series and year.

The histogram can also be customized in terms of size of boxes, whether a density function should be fit and displayed, together with a user specified smoothing factor.
